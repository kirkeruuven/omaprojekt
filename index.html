What is Material Design?

Material Design is a design system built and supported by Google designers and developers. Material.io includes in-depth UX guidance and UI component implementations for Android, Flutter, and the Web.

Design Principles - Google Material Design

1. Material is the Metaphor

This principle is based on real-world physics. Material Design creates a digital environment that feels tangible and familiar by mimicking the behavior of physical objects like paper.

2. Bold, Graphic, and Intentional

Material Design emphasizes clarity through bold use of colors, typography, and white space.

3. Motion Provides Meaning

Motion is used to guide users through the interface. Transitions are smooth, intentional, and focus on user actions.

4. Adaptive Design for All Screens

Material Design is responsive and works seamlessly on various screen sizes and devices.

5. Consistent Components

Material Design components (like buttons, cards, and sliders) are standardized for consistency across apps and devices.
